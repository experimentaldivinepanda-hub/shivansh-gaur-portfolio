The Operator

A management student working at the intersection of finance, marketing, politics, and current affairs — and a believer that analytical rigor is what makes that intersection navigable rather than noisy.

As Vice President of NMIMS Bangalore's UG Placement Committee, Shivansh sat at the center of the placement engine — optimizing the process and the relationships between recruiters, students, and faculty that make it work. That same pragmatic, problem-first approach now shows up everywhere from a PepsiCo go-to-market apprenticeship to a national case-competition win built on an uncomfortable ethics question.

He writes in public. Three years of LinkedIn essays on careers, marketing psychology, and the strategy hiding inside the news cycle have built a following that treats him as a credible voice on personal branding and career growth — not bad for someone who's still finishing his BBA.
